Today marks an unexpected milestone in my life — my **first unemployed day after 11+ years of continuous professional work**.

To be honest, it felt strange.

The day was unusually quiet, and boredom hit harder than expected. But instead of letting the time slip away, I decided to convert this phase into something meaningful.

So I’m starting this blog.

## Why This Blog?

I’m currently in the middle of a transition phase, waiting for my onboarding and background verification clearance for an upcoming opportunity.

Until then, I want to stay consistent, sharpen my skills, and share the knowledge I’ve gained over the years with fellow engineers.

This blog will be my daily log of:

- Technical learnings and experiments  
- Architecture and system design thoughts  
- DevOps, cloud, and automation topics  
- Career reflections and engineering leadership notes  
- Hands-on implementation ideas and code snippets  

## About Me

I’m **Muhammed Swafuvan**, a technology professional with **11+ years of experience** in software engineering, cloud modernization, enterprise architecture, and engineering leadership.

I’ve worked across domains like:

- Healthcare ERP
- Maritime platforms
- Logistics and enterprise systems

My core strengths include:

- .NET Architecture and Enterprise System Design  
- Cloud (Azure / AWS) and DevOps automation  
- Docker, Kubernetes, CI/CD, Observability  
- Scalable and secure system modernization

## What’s Next?

From tomorrow onwards, I’ll begin publishing short daily posts — even if it’s small progress.

Consistency is the goal.

If you're an engineer, architect, or someone exploring modern software practices, feel free to follow along.

Let’s build, learn, and grow — one day at a time.
